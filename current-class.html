Unit conversions

After that, it has a section called "Next Steps" with a link to an assessment---but don't do that; instead, you can try the worksheets linked in the "Still Need Practice?" section.
  • Dimensional Analysis: Nice video walkthrough and some practice with unit conversions from Khan Academy.
